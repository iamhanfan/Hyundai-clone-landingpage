Hyundai is a South Korean multinational automotive manufacturer headquartered in Seoul. It is one of the largest and most well-known automobile manufacturers in the world. Hyundai Motor Company was founded in 1967 and has since become a major player in the global automotive industry. Hyundai produces a wide range of vehicles, including compact cars, sedans, SUVs, and electric vehicles. The company has gained a reputation for offering affordable and reliable vehicles with modern features and technology. Some popular Hyundai models include the Hyundai Sonata, Hyundai Elantra, Hyundai Tucson, Hyundai Santa Fe, and Hyundai Kona.
